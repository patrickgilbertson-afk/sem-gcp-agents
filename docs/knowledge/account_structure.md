# Account Structure

## Campaign Naming Conventions

**Format**: `{CampaignType}_{Theme}_{Geo}[_{Variant}]`

**Examples**:
- `NonBrand_AI-Code_US` - NonBrand campaign targeting AI code editor keywords in US
- `Brand_Core_UK` - Brand defense campaign in UK
- `Competitor_GitHub_DE` - Competitor conquest campaign targeting GitHub in Germany

## Campaign Themes

### NonBrand Campaigns
Campaigns targeting users searching for solutions (not brand-aware):
- **AI-Code**: AI-powered code editors, coding assistants
- **Developer-Tools**: General developer productivity tools
- **Cloud-IDE**: Cloud-based development environments

### Brand Campaigns
Campaigns targeting users searching for our brand:
- **Core**: Primary brand terms (exact company name)
- **Products**: Brand + product name combinations
- **Misspellings**: Common brand name misspellings

### Competitor Campaigns
Campaigns targeting users searching for competitors:
- **GitHub**: GitHub Copilot alternatives
- **Cursor**: Cursor AI alternatives
- **Tabnine**: Tabnine alternatives

## Geo Targeting Strategy

### Primary Geos (Tier 1)
- **US**: United States - largest market, highest budgets
- **UK**: United Kingdom - English-speaking, high-value
- **DE**: Germany - DACH region leader
- **FR**: France - Western Europe expansion

### Secondary Geos (Tier 2)
- **CA**: Canada - English-speaking North America
- **AU**: Australia - APAC English-speaking
- **NL**: Netherlands - Northern Europe expansion

## Budget Allocation

**By Campaign Type**:
- NonBrand: 60% of total budget (acquisition focus)
- Brand: 30% of total budget (impression share defense)
- Competitor: 10% of total budget (opportunistic)

**By Geo**:
- US: 50% of campaign budget
- UK: 20% of campaign budget
- DE: 15% of campaign budget
- FR: 10% of campaign budget
- Other: 5% of campaign budget

## Sync Group Strategy

### SYNCED Management
Campaigns in the same sync group share keywords and ad copy across geos:
- **NonBrand campaigns**: All NonBrand campaigns with the same theme (e.g., NonBrand_AI-Code_*) are synced
- **Brand campaigns**: All Brand campaigns with the same theme are synced
- Changes to keywords/ads in one geo propagate to all geos in the sync group

### INDIVIDUAL Management
Each campaign is evaluated independently:
- **Competitor campaigns**: Each competitor has unique messaging and targeting
- Budget and bid adjustments are geo-specific

## Performance Targets

**NonBrand**:
- Target CPA: $80-$120 (varies by geo)
- Target ROAS: 400%+
- Quality Score: 7+ average

**Brand**:
- Impression Share: 90%+ (top of page)
- CTR: 20%+ (branded keywords)
- CPA: <$40 (highly efficient)

**Competitor**:
- CPA: $120-$180 (higher than NonBrand, but acceptable for conquest)
- Conversion Rate: 3-5%
- Quality Score: 5-7 average (expected lower QS on competitor terms)
