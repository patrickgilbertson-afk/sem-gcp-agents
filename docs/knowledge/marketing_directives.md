## Brand Voice Guidelines

**Tone**:
- Professional but friendly
- Technical but accessible
- Confident but not arrogant

**Do**:
- Use developer language ("build", "ship", "debug")
- Lead with specific benefits ("10x faster", "40% more efficient")
- Show, don't tell (code examples, demos)
- Speak to pain points (context switching, boilerplate code, bugs)

**Don't**:
- Use overly salesy language ("act now", "limited time")
- Make unrealistic claims
- Talk down to developers
- Use generic marketing speak

**Example Good Headlines**:
- "Debug 3x faster with AI-powered error detection"
- "Ship features, not boilerplate"
- "Your codebase, supercharged"

**Example Bad Headlines**:
- "The world's best AI coding tool!" (too salesy)
- "Revolutionary breakthrough in software development" (too generic)
- "Don't miss out - sign up today!" (too pushy)

## Messaging Hierarchy

**Level 1 (Primary)**: Speed/Productivity
- "Code 10x faster"
- "Ship features in hours, not days"

**Level 2 (Secondary)**: Quality
- "Write better code with AI assistance"
- "Catch bugs before they reach production"

**Level 3 (Supporting)**: Collaboration
- "Real-time pair programming with AI"
- "Your team's collective knowledge, always available"

## Restricted Terms

**Do NOT use**:
- "AGI" or "General AI" (overpromising)
- "Replaces developers" (bad optics, not our position)
- Competitor names in ad copy (trademark issues)
- Medical/legal/financial use cases (liability concerns)

## Target Personas

### Individual Developer
- **Age**: 25-40
- **Title**: Software Engineer, Full-Stack Developer
- **Pain Points**: Repetitive tasks, context switching, learning new frameworks
- **Motivation**: Personal productivity, career advancement
- **Message**: Speed, learning, quality

### Engineering Manager
- **Age**: 30-50
- **Title**: Engineering Manager, VP Engineering, CTO
- **Pain Points**: Team velocity, code quality, hiring challenges
- **Motivation**: Team productivity, cost efficiency, time to market
- **Message**: Team efficiency, ROI, retention

### Enterprise Buyer
- **Age**: 35-55
- **Title**: CIO, Director of Engineering, VP of IT
- **Pain Points**: Security, compliance, scaling, vendor management
- **Motivation**: Risk mitigation, standardization, cost control
- **Message**: Security, compliance, enterprise support

## A/B Testing Priorities

**Currently Testing**:

- Free trial vs. discount offer CTAs

**Winning Variants**:
- "10x faster" beats "better code" by 23%
